Required Materials

  • Application
  • Official Transcripts
  • GRE/GMAT/MAT scores as required by the academic department
  • Application fee of $60
  • Materials requested by the academic department (e.g. letters of recommendation)

Minimum Requirements

  • Baccalaureate degree or equivalent
  • GPA of 2.5
  • GRE/GMAT/MAT scores - variable by situation
